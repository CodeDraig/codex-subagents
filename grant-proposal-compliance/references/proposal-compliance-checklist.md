# Proposal Compliance Checklist

- Capture sponsor, opportunity number, version date, submission portal, deadline, and amendment history.
- Extract eligibility, required components, attachments, forms, certifications, signatures, page limits, font and margin rules.
- Map every requirement to proposal owner, location, status, evidence, and unresolved question.
- Track review criteria, scoring weights, budget restrictions, data-management requirements, sharing requirements, and reporting expectations.
- Escalate submission authority, legal terms, institutional certifications, cost share, and restricted budget decisions.
