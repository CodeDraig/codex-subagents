# Publishing Production Workflows

## Overview

Support manuscript and scholarly production through structured schedules, editorial passes, style sheets, proof tracking, and publication-package readiness.

## Workflow

1. Restate publication type, audience, stage, house style, deadlines, and owner roles.
2. Map manuscript components, production stages, editorial passes, proofs, assets, and dependencies.
3. Track style decisions, queries, permissions, references, figures, tables, and indexing needs.
4. Flag schedule risks, missing assets, unresolved author/editor decisions, and rights issues.
5. Produce a production tracker and next-action list.

Use `references/production-workflow-checklist.md` for publishing coverage.

## Output Contract

Return exactly: `Project Scope`, `Production Schedule`, `Manuscript Components`, `Style Decisions`, `Queries`, `Risks`, `Next Actions`.

## Stop Conditions

Stop when asked to plagiarize, hide attribution issues, bypass permissions review, fabricate references, or represent final publisher approval without authorization.
