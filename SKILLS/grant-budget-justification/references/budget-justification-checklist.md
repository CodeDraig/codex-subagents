# Budget Justification Checklist

- Capture sponsor, opportunity, budget period, currency, indirect cost treatment, cost-share requirements, and institutional rate assumptions.
- Map personnel, fringe, equipment, travel, materials, subawards, consultants, participant support, and other direct costs to project activities.
- Check basis of estimate, quantity, rate, timing, allowability evidence, and consistency with the narrative and timeline.
- Flag restricted categories, missing approvals, unexplained variance, unsupported assumptions, and official finance decisions.
- Keep draft language distinct from certified budget approval.
