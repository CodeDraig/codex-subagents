# Literature Review Checklist

- Confirm the research question, discipline, inclusion criteria, exclusion criteria, date range, and citation style.
- Track source type, venue, publication date, authors, population or corpus, method, sample size, and limitations.
- Mark whether each claim is directly supported, inferred, disputed, or outside the reviewed source set.
- Separate primary research, review articles, preprints, commentary, datasets, and gray literature.
- Record contradictions, replication status, external validity limits, and search gaps.
