# Legislative Tracking Checklist

- Capture jurisdiction, chamber, bill number, topic, sponsor, committee, session, and official source URL.
- Track introductions, amendments, committee actions, hearings, votes, referrals, deadlines, enactment, veto, and effective dates.
- Note source timestamp, version, summary changes, affected stakeholders, implementation implications, and open questions.
- Distinguish official text from summaries, lobbying materials, press coverage, and speculation.
- Escalate legal interpretation, compliance obligations, and policy-position decisions.
