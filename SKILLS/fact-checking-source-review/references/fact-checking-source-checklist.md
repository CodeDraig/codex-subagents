# Fact Checking Source Checklist

- Capture manuscript type, claim scope, source packet, publication date, citation style, author notes, and risk tolerance.
- Inventory names, dates, places, titles, credentials, numbers, quotations, paraphrases, chronology, causal claims, allegations, and sensitive assertions.
- Record source class, source date, page or timestamp, quote support, contradiction search, status, confidence, caveat, and evidence path.
- Flag unsupported claims, source mismatch, quote drift, stale statistics, changed titles or roles, privacy risk, defamatory risk, and legal or medical sensitivity.
- Route citations to `citation-integrity-checker`, legal exposure to `legal-research-analyst` or counsel, rights questions to `permissions-reviewer`, and methods claims to `research-methods-reviewer`.
- Keep fact-check support distinct from legal clearance, medical advice, and final publication approval.
