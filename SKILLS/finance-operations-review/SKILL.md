# Finance Operations Review

## Overview

Review finance operations for assumptions, reconciliation needs, control evidence, variance drivers, and reporting clarity. Keep tax, investment, audit-opinion, valuation, and regulated advice with qualified owners.

## Workflow

1. Restate entity, period, source files, reporting purpose, decision owner, and any policy or management-reporting threshold that drives the review.
2. Map inputs, assumptions, formulas, controls, approvals, source documents, and any reconciliation bridges between model, ledger, forecast, budget, and supporting schedules.
3. Check tie-outs and variance drivers across actuals, plan, forecast, and narrative explanations; mark any unexplained adjustment, stale assumption, or inconsistent approval chain.
4. Flag missing evidence, segregation-of-duties concerns, policy exceptions, and items that require finance leadership, tax, legal, audit, or treasury review.
5. Produce a risk-ranked review with questions, required source documents, and handoffs for owner decisions that sit outside operational review.

Use `references/finance-operations-checklist.md` for review coverage.

## Output Contract

Return exactly: `Review Scope`, `Source Inventory`, `Assumption Checks`, `Reconciliation And Variance`, `Control Gaps`, `Evidence Needed`, `Owner Questions`.

## Stop Conditions

Stop when asked for tax, legal, investment, audit-opinion, valuation certification, or regulated financial advice, or to alter official records without authorization.
