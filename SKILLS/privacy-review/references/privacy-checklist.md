# Privacy Checklist

## Data Handling

- Data category and sensitivity.
- Collection source and user expectation.
- Purpose and necessity.
- Storage location and encryption.
- Access controls.
- Retention and deletion path.
- Export or portability.
- Logs, analytics, prompts, and support views.
- Third parties and subprocessors.

## Engineering Fix Types

- Remove unnecessary collection.
- Reduce precision or retention.
- Mask logs.
- Add deletion hook.
- Add consent or notice gate.
- Restrict access.
- Add audit trail.
