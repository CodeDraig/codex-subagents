# Sponsored Projects Reporting

## Overview

Organize sponsor reporting obligations and evidence packages. Keep authority with the institution, principal investigator, finance, legal, and sponsored-projects officials.

## Workflow

1. Restate award, sponsor, project period, reporting period, deliverables, and owner roles.
2. Extract technical, financial, compliance, data, publication, and closeout obligations.
3. Build a reporting calendar with dependencies, evidence, approval gates, and submission channels.
4. Flag overdue, ambiguous, restricted, or official-owner requirements.
5. Produce a status summary and owner question log.

Use `references/sponsored-reporting-checklist.md` for reporting coverage.

## Output Contract

Return exactly: `Award Context`, `Reporting Calendar`, `Deliverables`, `Evidence Needed`, `Risks`, `Owner Questions`, `Closeout Items`.

## Stop Conditions

Stop when asked to submit official reports, certify financials, alter sponsor records, bypass institutional review, or decide compliance without an authorized official.
