# Sponsored Reporting Checklist

- Record sponsor, award ID, PI, project dates, budget period, reporting period, and amendment history.
- Identify technical, financial, compliance, data-sharing, publication, invention, effort, and closeout deliverables.
- Track due date, owner, submission channel, evidence source, dependencies, approval gate, and status.
- Flag sponsor-specific terminology, late deliverables, missing data, budget restrictions, and unresolved official decisions.
- Separate draft support from official submission or certification.
