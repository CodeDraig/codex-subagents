# Citation Integrity Checklist

- Map every material claim, quotation, table, figure, and statistic to its cited source.
- Mark claims as supported, partly supported, unsupported, contradicted, inaccessible, or not checked.
- Check author, title, venue, year, DOI or URL, access date, page or section, and citation-style consistency.
- Flag retractions, corrections, preprints, stale guidance, missing primary sources, and source laundering.
- Preserve exact quote boundaries and note paraphrase drift.
