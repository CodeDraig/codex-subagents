# Line Copyediting Checklist

- Capture edit level, audience, house style, dialect, file format, tracked-change expectations, and author voice constraints.
- Check grammar, punctuation, spelling, capitalization, hyphenation, numerals, citations, headings, lists, tables, figure callouts, and cross-references.
- Review clarity, concision, rhythm, transitions, repetition, jargon, inclusive language, dialogue punctuation, and point-of-view or tense consistency.
- Maintain style-sheet entries for names, places, invented terms, abbreviations, measurements, chronology, capitalization, spelling variants, and exceptions.
- Query ambiguity, factual uncertainty, continuity conflicts, missing references, permissions issues, and author-owned choices.
- Separate applied edits, recommended edits, proof blockers, and downstream handoffs.
