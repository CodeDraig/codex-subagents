# SOW Review Checklist

- Capture parties, term, governing agreement, pricing, assumptions, exclusions, and change process.
- Extract deliverables, milestones, dependencies, acceptance criteria, service levels, staffing, access, and responsibilities.
- Check ambiguity, inconsistent dates, missing owners, payment triggers, termination effects, IP/data/security mentions, and operational feasibility.
- Flag specialist reviews for legal, procurement, finance, privacy, security, accessibility, and technical delivery.
- Separate issue spotting from contract approval.
