# Fiction Development Checklist

- Capture format, genre, audience, target length, manuscript stage, point of view, comp titles, series context, and author goals.
- Map premise, protagonist desire, stakes, opposition, turning points, midpoint, climax, resolution, and promised payoff.
- Track scene purpose, conflict, change, information release, pacing, emotional beat, and chapter-to-chapter propulsion.
- Check character motivation, arc progression, relationship dynamics, dialogue function, voice consistency, and point-of-view discipline.
- Record continuity issues for timeline, setting rules, canon, invented terms, names, ages, geography, and unresolved setup.
- Separate structural blockers, revision opportunities, line-edit notes, and author-only creative decisions.
