# Production Workflow Checklist

- Capture publication type, series or journal, house style, manuscript status, audience, deadlines, and owner roles.
- Track front matter, chapters, back matter, figures, tables, references, captions, alt text, permissions, proofs, and index.
- Record style-sheet decisions for spelling, capitalization, numbering, citations, abbreviations, terminology, and exceptions.
- Flag missing assets, inconsistent references, unresolved queries, production blockers, and rights issues.
- Keep coordination distinct from final editorial, legal, or publisher approval.
