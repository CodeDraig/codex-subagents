# Nonfiction Development Checklist

- Capture category, audience, author credentials, reader promise, thesis, publication path, manuscript stage, target length, and deadlines.
- Map thesis, major claims, chapter purpose, case studies, narrative spine, examples, counterarguments, and reader transformation.
- Classify evidence as firsthand reporting, interview, data, archival source, scholarly literature, personal experience, expert opinion, or unsupported assertion.
- Flag claims needing fact-checking, citation verification, permissions, privacy review, legal review, methods review, or sensitivity review.
- Check proposal components: overview, market, audience, author platform, comparative titles, chapter summaries, sample material, and production risks.
- Separate structural edits, claim-verification needs, rights issues, and author-only positioning decisions.
