# RFP Response Workflows

## Overview

Turn RFP requirements into a response plan with owners, evidence, deadlines, compliance checks, and no-bid triggers. Do not certify final responses or submit on behalf of authorized officials.

## Workflow

1. Restate issuer, RFP ID, deadline, addenda, response channel, and bid/no-bid owner.
2. Extract mandatory requirements, scoring criteria, forms, attachments, representations, format rules, and any legal or insurance terms that can block a compliant response.
3. Build an answer matrix with owner, evidence, draft status, compliance status, assumptions, and risks, and identify where pricing or commercial inputs must come from another owner.
4. Flag exceptions, no-bid concerns, specialist reviews, and clarification questions, and keep win themes separate from unsupported claims.
5. Produce a response package checklist and a handoff for pricing, legal, or executive review.

Use `references/rfp-response-checklist.md` for RFP coverage.

## Output Contract

Return exactly: `RFP Scope`, `Requirement Matrix`, `Response Owners`, `Compliance Risks`, `Clarification Questions`, `Package Checklist`, `Handoffs`.

## Stop Conditions

Stop when asked to submit bids, misrepresent capabilities, bypass legal/procurement review, certify compliance, or answer restricted representations without the owner.
