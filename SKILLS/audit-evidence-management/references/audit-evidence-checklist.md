# Audit Evidence Checklist

- Capture audit scope, period, assertion, control ID, request ID, owner, due date, and reviewer.
- Track source system, artifact path, preparer, reviewer, extraction date, population, sample, and retention needs.
- Map evidence to control objective, procedure, test attribute, exception, remediation, and management response.
- Flag missing approvals, stale screenshots, unverifiable exports, inconsistent totals, restricted data, and privilege concerns.
- Keep evidence organization distinct from auditor or management certification.
