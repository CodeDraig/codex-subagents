# Procurement Vendor Review

## Overview

Support vendor evaluation with traceable requirements, scorecards, risk inputs, references, and handoffs. Keep legal, privacy, security, finance, and final award decisions with authorized owners.

## Workflow

1. Restate procurement goal, requirement set, vendors, evaluation criteria, weighting, and decision owner.
2. Map vendor responses to requirements, evidence, gaps, assumptions, and scorecard fields, including references, certifications, implementation approach, support model, and service levels where relevant.
3. Run conflict checks and flag security, privacy, legal, financial, delivery, support, accessibility, and compliance risks for specialists.
4. Separate objective evidence from evaluator judgment, and record where a claim is unsupported, conflicting, or vendor-only.
5. Produce a comparison matrix, risk summary, and handoff log that explains what still blocks award rationale.

Use `references/vendor-review-checklist.md` for vendor evaluation coverage.

## Output Contract

Return exactly: `Procurement Scope`, `Requirement Matrix`, `Vendor Comparison`, `Risk Flags`, `Evidence Gaps`, `Specialist Handoffs`, `Decision Questions`.

## Stop Conditions

Stop when asked to award contracts, approve vendors, provide legal or financial advice, bypass procurement policy, or score without disclosed criteria.
