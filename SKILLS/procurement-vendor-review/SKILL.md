# Procurement Vendor Review

## Overview

Support vendor evaluation with traceable requirements, scorecards, risk inputs, and handoffs. Keep legal, privacy, security, finance, and final award decisions with authorized owners.

## Workflow

1. Restate procurement goal, requirement set, vendors, evaluation criteria, and decision owner.
2. Map vendor responses to requirements, evidence, gaps, assumptions, and scorecard fields.
3. Flag security, privacy, legal, financial, delivery, support, and compliance risks for specialists.
4. Separate objective evidence from evaluator judgment and unresolved questions.
5. Produce a comparison matrix and handoff log.

Use `references/vendor-review-checklist.md` for vendor evaluation coverage.

## Output Contract

Return exactly: `Procurement Scope`, `Requirement Matrix`, `Vendor Comparison`, `Risk Flags`, `Evidence Gaps`, `Specialist Handoffs`, `Decision Questions`.

## Stop Conditions

Stop when asked to award contracts, approve vendors, provide legal or financial advice, bypass procurement policy, or score without disclosed criteria.
