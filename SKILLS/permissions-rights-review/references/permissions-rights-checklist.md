# Permissions Rights Checklist

- Capture content title, creator, source, owner, intended use, format, territory, language, edition, print run, term, and platform.
- Track license type, permission request, grant, fee, restrictions, attribution language, credit line, expiration, and evidence path.
- Check whether transformations, excerpts, images, tables, data, cover use, marketing use, and digital use are covered.
- Flag unclear ownership, missing evidence, incompatible terms, orphan works, public-domain uncertainty, and legal-review needs.
- Separate tracking from legal clearance.
