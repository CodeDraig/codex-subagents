# Clause Review Checklist

## Contract Basics

- Parties, affiliates, signatories, and effective date.
- Version, amendment, exhibit, order form, or statement of work identifiers.
- Governing law, venue, notice contacts, and defined terms.
- Review purpose and clauses in scope.

## Clause Coverage

- Scope, deliverables, acceptance, dependencies, and change control.
- Payment, invoicing, taxes, expenses, credits, and audit rights.
- Term, renewal, termination, suspension, transition, and survival.
- Confidentiality, data protection, security, privacy, and subprocessors.
- Intellectual property, licenses, ownership, open source, and deliverables.
- Liability, indemnity, warranties, disclaimers, insurance, and remedies.
- Compliance, records, reporting, assignment, subcontracting, and order of precedence.

## Escalation

- Ambiguous or conflicting language.
- Missing attachments, definitions, exhibits, or approval authority.
- Legal, privacy, security, procurement, finance, or operational owner questions.
- No legal advice or approval decision.
