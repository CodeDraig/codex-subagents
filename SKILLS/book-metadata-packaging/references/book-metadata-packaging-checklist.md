# Book Metadata Packaging Checklist

- Capture title, subtitle, author, contributors, edition, series, ISBN, formats, publication date, publisher, price, territory, audience, and category.
- Align metadata across manuscript, cover, title page, copyright page, ISBN record, retailer fields, distributor sheet, catalog copy, and website copy.
- Prepare or review short description, long description, back-cover copy, author bio, comp titles, tagline, keywords, category rationale, and launch blurbs.
- Check endorsements, awards, credentials, sales claims, rights restrictions, age/category fit, sensitive content flags, accessibility metadata, and alt text.
- Flag title conflicts, inconsistent contributor names, unverifiable claims, missing permissions, inaccessible assets, and platform-specific blockers.
- Separate discoverability recommendations from guaranteed sales, retailer approval, rights clearance, and final publication authorization.
