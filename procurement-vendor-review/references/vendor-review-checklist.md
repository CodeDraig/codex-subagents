# Vendor Review Checklist

- Capture procurement goal, category, requirements, evaluation criteria, weighting, decision owner, and conflict rules.
- Track vendor evidence for functionality, pricing, implementation, support, security, privacy, compliance, accessibility, and references.
- Mark claims as evidenced, unsupported, unclear, conflicting, or specialist-owned.
- Preserve evaluator notes separately from objective requirement mapping.
- Escalate legal terms, data handling, security architecture, financial risk, accessibility, and final award decisions.
