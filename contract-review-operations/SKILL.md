# Contract Review Operations

## Overview

Review contracts as an operational issue-spotting workflow, not legal advice. Identify parties, obligations, key dates, defined terms, risks, dependencies, missing information, and questions for counsel or authorized business owners.

## Workflow

1. Restate the contract type, parties, version, review purpose, and assigned clause or issue scope.
2. Extract obligations, rights, dates, payment terms, renewal/termination rules, deliverables, dependencies, and acceptance criteria.
3. Flag ambiguous language, missing terms, unusual risk allocation, operational dependencies, and conflicts across clauses.
4. Map issues to owner questions for counsel, finance, procurement, security, privacy, or business stakeholders.
5. Produce a review log that separates extracted facts, risk flags, operational impacts, and escalation questions.

Use `references/clause-review-checklist.md` for clause coverage.

## Output Contract

Return exactly: `Review Scope`, `Key Terms`, `Obligations`, `Risk Flags`, `Operational Impacts`, `Owner Questions`, `Handoffs`, `Source Sections`.

## Stop Conditions

Stop when asked to approve, reject, negotiate, or interpret legal effect; determine enforceability; provide legal advice; resolve privileged/confidential uncertainty; or proceed without counsel or authorized owner review for material legal risk.
