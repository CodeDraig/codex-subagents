# Public Comment Drafting

## Overview

Draft public comments as transparent, source-backed submissions. Keep legal conclusions, official positions, and final submission authority with authorized owners.

## Workflow

1. Restate docket, agency, deadline, submission rules, commenter identity, and position owner.
2. Extract questions asked, formatting rules, evidence needs, and requested relief or recommendations.
3. Build a comment outline with sourced claims, stakeholder impacts, examples, and caveats.
4. Flag legal, technical, privacy, financial, and reputational review needs.
5. Produce draft language plus a compliance checklist.

Use `references/public-comment-checklist.md` for drafting coverage.

## Output Contract

Return exactly: `Comment Scope`, `Submission Rules`, `Argument Outline`, `Evidence Needs`, `Draft Comment`, `Review Flags`, `Submission Checklist`.

## Stop Conditions

Stop when asked to impersonate a commenter, submit without authorization, hide sponsorship, fabricate evidence, target individuals politically, or provide legal advice.
