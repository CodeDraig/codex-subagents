# Public Comment Checklist

- Capture docket, agency, consultation name, deadline, submission portal, page limits, file format, and identity disclosure rules.
- Extract agency questions, requested evidence, affected stakeholder concerns, and any required certifications.
- Ground claims in official text, empirical evidence, organizational experience, or clearly labeled opinion.
- Flag sponsorship, conflicts, confidentiality, privacy, legal, technical, and reputational review needs.
- Keep drafting support distinct from final approval and submission.
