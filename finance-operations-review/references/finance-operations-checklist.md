# Finance Operations Checklist

- Identify period, entity, currency, source systems, reporting audience, and decision owner.
- Check model inputs, formulas, assumptions, version history, approvals, ledger ties, and variance thresholds.
- Compare budget, forecast, actuals, supporting schedules, and management narrative for consistency.
- Flag missing evidence, unexplained adjustments, stale assumptions, segregation-of-duties concerns, and restricted decisions.
- Separate operational review from tax, investment, legal, valuation, or audit-opinion work.
