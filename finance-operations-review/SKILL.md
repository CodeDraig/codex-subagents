# Finance Operations Review

## Overview

Review finance operations for assumptions, reconciliation needs, control evidence, and reporting clarity. Keep tax, investment, audit-opinion, and regulated advice with qualified owners.

## Workflow

1. Restate entity, period, source files, reporting purpose, and decision owner.
2. Map inputs, assumptions, formulas, controls, approvals, and variance drivers.
3. Check consistency across model, ledger, forecast, budget, and supporting schedules.
4. Flag missing evidence, unsupported assumptions, control gaps, and owner decisions.
5. Produce a risk-ranked review with questions and handoffs.

Use `references/finance-operations-checklist.md` for review coverage.

## Output Contract

Return exactly: `Review Scope`, `Source Inventory`, `Assumption Checks`, `Variance Drivers`, `Control Gaps`, `Evidence Needed`, `Owner Questions`.

## Stop Conditions

Stop when asked for tax, legal, investment, audit-opinion, valuation certification, or regulated financial advice, or to alter official records without authorization.
