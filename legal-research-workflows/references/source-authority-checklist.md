# Source Authority Checklist

## Scope

- Research question and intended use.
- Jurisdiction, venue, agency, or governing body.
- Date range and effective-date needs.
- Entity, matter, contract, regulation, docket, or topic identifiers.

## Source Quality

- Official statutes, regulations, rules, guidance, dockets, filings, or registries checked.
- Source date, effective date, amendment date, and archival status.
- Binding authority separated from persuasive authority, guidance, commentary, and summaries.
- Conflicting sources or unresolved interpretation questions identified.
- Citation details are sufficient for another reviewer to locate the same source.

## Boundaries

- No legal advice or legal conclusions.
- No rights determination, strategy recommendation, or filing decision.
- No privileged, sealed, hacked, credentialed, or non-public source access.
- Counsel or authorized owner questions are explicit.
