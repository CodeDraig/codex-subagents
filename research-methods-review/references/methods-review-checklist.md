# Methods Review Checklist

- Confirm question, design, population, sampling frame, controls, data source, and planned analysis.
- Check construct validity, internal validity, external validity, bias, confounding, missingness, and measurement drift.
- Record ethics, consent, privacy, data-use, and preregistration requirements when visible.
- Verify reproducibility materials: protocol, code, environment, data dictionary, transformations, seeds, and version identifiers.
- Separate fatal blockers from fixable clarity issues and reviewer questions.
