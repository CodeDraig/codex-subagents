# Retention Workflow Checklist

## Inventory

- Record class, system, repository, owner, custodian, and format.
- Date range, retention trigger, jurisdiction, and business process.
- Related contracts, policies, regulations, or retention schedule entries.
- Duplicate, derivative, backup, export, or archive copies.

## Constraints

- Legal hold, investigation, audit, dispute, or preservation notice.
- Privacy, security, confidentiality, privilege, or access restriction.
- Minimum retention, maximum retention, deletion, anonymization, and export requirements.
- Approval authority and evidence required for disposition.

## Workflow

- Preserve, review, retain, dispose, archive, or escalate decision path.
- Audit trail, reviewer, approver, timestamp, and source policy captured.
- Questions for counsel, records management, privacy, security, or business owners.
