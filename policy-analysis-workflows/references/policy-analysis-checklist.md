# Policy Analysis Checklist

- Capture jurisdiction, agency or legislative body, instrument type, version date, effective dates, and audience.
- Inventory official text, explanatory materials, fiscal notes, agency guidance, research, stakeholder comments, and implementation evidence.
- Map goals, mechanisms, affected populations, compliance burdens, costs, benefits, equity impacts, operational dependencies, and uncertainty.
- Separate neutral analysis, advocacy language, legal interpretation, and political strategy.
- Flag source gaps, outdated sources, stakeholder blind spots, and specialist handoffs.
