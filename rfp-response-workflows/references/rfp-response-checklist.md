# RFP Response Checklist

- Capture issuer, RFP ID, version, addenda, deadlines, submission portal, required format, and Q&A window.
- Extract mandatory requirements, evaluation criteria, pricing forms, legal terms, certifications, insurance, references, and attachments.
- Map each answer to owner, source evidence, draft status, reviewer, risk, and final approver.
- Flag exceptions, conflicts, unsupported claims, legal terms, security or privacy requirements, and bid/no-bid concerns.
- Keep draft response support distinct from official submission.
