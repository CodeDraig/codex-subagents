# Invoice Reconciliation Checklist

- Capture vendor, invoice number, PO, receipt, contract, approval chain, payment terms, currency, and period.
- Match quantities, unit prices, discounts, tax, freight, totals, dates, bank or payment status, and coding.
- Flag duplicates, missing receipts, price variance, quantity variance, missing approvals, vendor master mismatch, and policy exceptions.
- Record owner, status, evidence path, aging, and next action.
- Separate reconciliation support from payment authorization.
