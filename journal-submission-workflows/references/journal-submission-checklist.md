# Journal Submission Checklist

- Capture journal, article type, instructions date, submission stage, corresponding author, ORCID, deadline, and portal.
- Track manuscript file, title page, abstract, keywords, references, figures, tables, supplements, cover letter, highlights, graphical abstract, and data availability.
- Check reporting guidelines, ethics approval, consent, conflicts, funding, author contributions, permissions, and disclosure statements.
- For revisions, map each reviewer point to response, manuscript location, evidence, and unresolved author decision.
- Keep package preparation distinct from official submission and research integrity certification.
